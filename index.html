    How to read these colors

    Each color shows how your 2026 appraised value compares with the median of 5 similar homes (same neighborhood, same HCAD grade, within ±15% square footage and ±10 years of age).

    • Strong case — more than 7% over the median. Under Tex. Tax Code §41.43(b)(3), the ARB is required to lower your appraisal to that median if your comps hold up.
    • Consider filing — 2–7% over. A reduction is possible, but the ARB panel may push back on individual comp choices.
    • Skip — less than 2% over. Within the noise of comp selection; not worth filing.
