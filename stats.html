    How this was computed

    Every single-family parcel in Jersey Village (HCAD tax district 070) is compared against the median $/sqft of its 5 closest comparable neighbors — same HCAD neighborhood code, same grade, within ±15% of its living area, and within ±10 years of its build year. The resulting over-assessment percentage drives the color bucket and every headline figure above. Full methodology lives in CLAUDE.md alongside the source code.
